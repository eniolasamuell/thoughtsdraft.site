1. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through Thoughts Draft, including when you visit pages on the website, read articles or stories, contact us through available forms, subscribe to updates if subscription functionality is available, or interact with advertisements, analytics tools, and embedded third-party services connected to the website.

This policy is intended to cover the website experience as a whole, including publicly accessible content pages, story pages, informational pages, and any communication submitted through the website.

Important Note

Thoughts Draft is primarily a content and storytelling website. We do not ask readers to create accounts to read stories, and we do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information from casual visitors.

2. Information We Collect

The type of information we may collect depends on how you interact with the website. In general, this may include information you provide directly and information collected automatically through your browser or device when visiting the site.

2.1 Information You Voluntarily Provide

If you contact us through a contact form, email, or another communication channel available on the website, we may collect the information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content or inquiry details
  • Any additional information you include in your communication

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit Thoughts Draft, certain information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies, hosting logs, analytics services, cookies, and advertising tools. This may include:

  • IP address or approximate location information derived from IP
  • Browser type and browser version
  • Device type, operating system, and screen information
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, referral sources, and navigation patterns
  • Date and time of visits
  • General website performance and usage data

2.3 Information from Cookies and Similar Technologies

We and our third-party service providers may use cookies, tags, scripts, and similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site, remember preferences, improve performance, and support advertising features.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected through Thoughts Draft may be used for legitimate website operation, communication, content delivery, analytics, security, and advertising purposes. Depending on the nature of the information, we may use it to:

  • Respond to questions, feedback, business inquiries, or support messages sent through our contact channels
  • Maintain, improve, and optimize the website’s design, content, usability, and performance
  • Understand how readers interact with our stories and pages
  • Monitor traffic trends, engagement patterns, and general site analytics
  • Protect the website against abuse, spam, fraud, malicious activity, or technical misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable
  • Display advertisements and measure advertising performance through approved advertising partners

Communication

To reply to inquiries, messages, and feedback submitted by readers or visitors.

Website Improvement

To understand traffic patterns, improve page performance, and refine the overall reading experience.

Advertising Support

To support responsible advertising display and measure ad-related performance where permitted.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser or device when you visit a website. They are commonly used to make websites function properly, remember certain preferences, understand usage behavior, and support advertising or analytics features.

4.1 How Cookies May Be Used on Thoughts Draft

Cookies and related technologies may be used on this website to:

  • Remember basic site preferences or browsing behavior
  • Measure traffic and page performance
  • Understand which stories or pages readers engage with most
  • Support ad delivery, frequency management, and reporting
  • Help maintain website security and reduce spam or misuse

4.2 Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to review, block, or delete cookies through browser settings. If you choose to disable cookies, some parts of the website or certain advertising-related functions may not work as intended.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising Disclosure

Thoughts Draft may display advertising through Google AdSense and other approved advertising technologies. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on a user’s previous visits to this website and/or other websites on the internet.

5.1 Personalized and Non-Personalized Advertising

Depending on your location, browser settings, consent choices, and Google’s advertising systems, ads displayed on the site may be personalized or non-personalized. Personalized ads may use information such as browsing activity, general interests, or prior website interactions to show ads that may be more relevant to you.

5.2 Google Advertising Cookies

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads to users based on visits to Thoughts Draft and/or other websites. These technologies may help with ad delivery, frequency capping, reporting, fraud prevention, and measuring campaign effectiveness.

5.3 Your Advertising Choices

You may be able to manage or opt out of certain personalized advertising experiences by adjusting your browser settings, reviewing your consent options where available, or visiting Google’s advertising settings and privacy controls.

Advertising Transparency

Thoughts Draft does not personally create targeted advertising profiles about individual readers. However, third-party advertising partners such as Google may use automated systems, cookies, and similar technologies in connection with ad delivery on the website, subject to their own privacy practices and policies.

6. Third-Party Services

To operate and improve the website, Thoughts Draft may rely on third-party tools or providers. These may include services for website hosting, traffic analytics, spam protection, embedded content, form delivery, and advertising.

Examples of third-party services that may interact with website data include:

  • Advertising providers such as Google AdSense
  • Analytics or traffic measurement tools
  • Website hosting or infrastructure providers
  • Embedded media or third-party content tools, where applicable
  • Contact form delivery, anti-spam, or security tools

These third-party services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy notices and operational requirements. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services they interact with through the website.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including responding to inquiries, maintaining records of communications, operating the website, improving services, resolving disputes, protecting the site, or complying with legal obligations where required.

The exact retention period may vary depending on the nature of the information, how it was collected, whether it is needed for operational or legal reasons, and whether it is controlled directly by Thoughts Draft or by an integrated third-party service provider.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information handled through the website. These efforts may include secure website configuration, limited access to communication data, hosting-level protections, and routine maintenance practices.

However, no method of transmitting data over the internet or storing data electronically is completely secure. As a result, while we strive to protect information appropriately, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices & Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and the laws that apply to you, you may have certain rights relating to your personal information. These rights can vary by jurisdiction, but may include the right to request access to information you provided directly, request correction of inaccurate information, or request deletion of information where appropriate and legally permissible.

You may also take certain privacy-related actions directly, including:

  • Managing cookie settings through your browser
  • Choosing whether to submit information through contact forms
  • Adjusting Google advertising preferences where available
  • Contacting us if you have a privacy-related question about information you submitted directly

If you contact us regarding a privacy request, we may need reasonable information to verify the request and respond appropriately.

10. Children’s Privacy

If you believe that a child has submitted personal information to us through the website without appropriate consent where required, please contact us so that we can review the situation and take appropriate action.

We are not responsible for the privacy, security, or content practices of third-party websites or services that are not controlled by Thoughts Draft.

When this page is updated, the “Last Updated” date at the top of the page will be revised accordingly. Your continued use of the website after changes are posted may be treated as acceptance of the updated policy where permitted by law.
